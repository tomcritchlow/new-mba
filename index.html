Doing the work isn’t enough

Learning to be strategic is difficult, mostly because no one teaches you how to do it. We think that being strategic is learning about strategy, reading fancy books and learning fancy frameworks.

But really, being strategic is simply working in context. Every business and every project exists in overlapping concentric circles of context: markets, operations, capital, people.

How we react to work across these overlapping contexts reflects how successful we are and how much impact we have.

To become a strategic thinker and lead with influence, it’s not enough to do the work, you have to learn how to do the work around the work.
The NEW MBA is a new executive education platform to teach practical business skills that will help you navigate the modern world of work, move work forward and expand your options.

It’s a free newsletter and eventually an education platform to help you work with more confidence, influence and agility.
