It's about the work around the work

We spend our early careers chasing skills - things that will help us do the work. But everyone reaches a point in their career where knowing how to do the work isn't enough.

We're told we need skills, but we're judged by our impact. And impact requires collaboration, leadership, communication.

To reach senior roles, to work with imapct, we need to get better at the work around the work. But how do you learn these skills?

The NEW MBA is for people who know how to do the work, but want to have more imapct by learning how to do the work around the work.
